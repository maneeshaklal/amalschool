D. ADMISSION POLICY

How to apply :

If parents wish to enroll their child in the school, they should apply to the principal in the prescribed form available in the school office.

DIRECT ADMISSION (AS PER CBSE )

  • TC and a copy of it attested by the principal.
  • IX/XI registration copies (2) attested by principal.
  • Progress report and its copy attested by principal.
  • Proof of residence.
Procedure
  • Candidate seeking admission should register their name in the preceding year November onwards
  • Admission will be on first come first basis.
  • Original birth certificate and two passport photographs of student should be produced at the time of admission
  • For admission to class other than LKG, the child must attend proficiency test and interview..
Age of Children:-

Age of children admitted to school at the beginning of the academic year, 1 st June should be as follows:-

Pre KG : 3 years.

LKG: 4 years and above.

UKG : 5 years and above.

Class 1 : 6 and above.

Other classes : According to the class to which admission is sought

I – IX

  • Admission to these classes will be only as per availability of seats
  • Candidates seeking admission should register their name in school office
  • An assessment test will be conducted before admission is given. Admission will be given only to those who maintain a standard equivalent to that particular class for
    which admission is sought.
  • Candidates who are eligible for admission after the test should produce original birth certificate and T.C from a recognized institution at the time of admission.

XI

  • Candidates seeking admission should apply in the prescribed form. Prospectus can be had from the office during working hours on payment of 100/- by cash.
  • The principal has the right to accept/reject any application without giving any reason. A rank list will be published on the basis of marks obtained in X std.
  • Admission will be on the basis of rank list.

The following original certificates should be produced at the time of admission

  • Pass certificate of class X from the Board last attended.
  • Mark sheet of class X from the Board last attended.
  • Transfer certificate from the institution last attended.
  • Conduct certificate from the institution last attended.

ABOUT US

Amal English School

Amal English School, one of the most progressive and prestigious institutions in Kerala was established in the year 1992, at Chammanur, Punnayurkulam, Thrissur District with a view to impart excellent modern education in a serene atmosphere for the students from all parts of society. The school has obtained A grade affiliation from Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi with affiliation No: 930203 (Visit www.cbse.nic.in for knowing affiliation details) as a Senior Secondary School.

Amal Eglish School, P.O. Chammanur, Punnayurkulam, Thrissur District was started with kindergarden classes in the year 1992. The school is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi. It aims at the all round development of pupils socially, physically and morally by offering secular education. It imparts instruction in a calm and peaceful atmosphere. The school is open to all castes and creeds and it functions purely on charitable basis. The three language formula is followed. English, Hindi, Malayalam or Arabic will be followed upto Std VIII. As far as possible we follow the text books prescribed by the National Council of Education Research and Training (NCERT). Besides we provide a series of co-curricular education in sports and games arts and crafts, drawing and painting, computer education etc. The school session is from 1st June to 31st March. We have co-education providing effective moral instruction and spiritual lessons based on human and social values. The school prepares pupils for All India Secondary School Examination (AISSE) and Senior School Certificate Examination conducted by CBSE Delhi. We have continuous and comprehensive assessment for all classes.

The school is situated at Chammanur nearly 8kms away from Kunnamkulam and 2kms nearer to Attupuram on Kunnamkulam Althara road (via) Cheruvathani. The managing committee, the staff and the parents of the pupils are dedicated themselves to nourish young men and women with good character and broad vision to lead a useful and purposeful life.
